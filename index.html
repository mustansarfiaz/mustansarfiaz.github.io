Research Background

Currently working as a staff research scientist at IBM Research, Abu Dhabi, UAE. Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) with Prof. Fahad Khan and Hisham Cholakkal at the Intelligent Visual Analytics Lab.

I received my Ph.D. from Kyungpook National University, Daegu, Republic of Korea in 2021, supervised by Prof. Soon Ki Jung. My Master's degree was from Sejong University in 2016, supervised by Prof. Sung Wook Baik.
