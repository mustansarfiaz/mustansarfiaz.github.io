About

I am currently working as a staff research scientist at IBM Research, Abu Dhabi, UAE. Before that, I was a postdoctoral researcher at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI) with Prof. Fahad Khan and Hisham Cholakkal at the Intelligent Visual Analytics Lab.

I received my Ph.D. from Kyungpook National University, Daegu, Republic of Korea in 2021, where I contributed as a researcher in the Virtual Reality laboratory, supervised by Prof. Soon Ki Jung. Prior to that, I pursued my Master's degree from Sejong University in 2016, supervised by Prof. Sung Wook Baik.
